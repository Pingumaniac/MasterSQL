Q1. Write a query to select the student ID, name, Mathematics.GPA as Math_GPA, English.GPA as English_GPA of the students who took both Mathematics and English.
# StudentID StudentName Math_GPA English_GPA
1 101 Socrates 2.33 1.67
2 102 Plato 4.33 1.67
3 103 Aristotle 4.33 2

Correct Answer: SELECT Mathematics.StudentID, Mathematics.StudentName, Mathematics.GPA AS Math_GPA, English.GPA AS English_GPA FROM Mathematics INNER JOIN English ON Mathematics.StudentID = English.StudentID;

Q4. Write a query to select the student ID, name, Mathematics.GPA as Math_GPA, English.GPA AS English.GPA of the students who took either Mathematics and English using LEFT JOIN, RIGHT JOIN, and UNION.

Q5. Write a query to select the average mathematics GPA of the students who took both Mathematics and English. Label the column as "average_gpa".
# average_gpa
1 3.663333257039388

Correct Answer: SELECT AVG(GPA) AS "average_gpa" FROM Mathematics WHERE StudentID IN (SELECT StudentID FROM English);
